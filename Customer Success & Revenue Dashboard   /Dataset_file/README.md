# ****Customer Success and Sales Performance Dataset****

## **Overview**

This dataset contains granular transaction-level data used for analyzing Customer Success and Sales Performance. It covers various dimensions of the customer lifecycle, from acquisition and product purchase to delivery performance and post-purchase satisfaction. The data is designed to identify drivers of customer happiness and operational efficiency.

## **Data Source**

This dataset is downloaded from Kaggel 

**Format**: CSV (Structured flat file)

Scope: 5,780 records across multiple states and product lines.

## **Data Structure & Key Segments**

The data spans three critical business domains:

**1. Sales & Revenue Metrics**
Focuses on the financial performance of products across different acquisition channels.

Key Metrics: Price, Units Sold, and Total Revenue.

**2. Logistics & Operational Data**
Tracks the efficiency of the supply chain and product fulfillment.

Key Metrics: State (Geography), Delivery Performance (On-time vs. Delayed), and Return status.

**3. Customer Experience & Retention**
Captures qualitative feedback and behavioral data.

Key Metrics: Customer Acquisition Type, Return Rate, and Customer Satisfaction Ratings.

Use Case: Correlating delivery performance with satisfaction levels (e.g., "How much does a 'delayed' delivery impact the satisfaction score?")
