# **Customer Success & Revenue Dashboard**

## **Overview**

This interactive dashboard provides a 360-degree view of business performance, integrating sales revenue with operational efficiency and customer experience metrics. By analyzing 5,780 transactions, the dashboard identifies the correlation between logistics (delivery timing), product quality (returns), and overall customer satisfaction.

## **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

1) Total Revenue: $9,585,868

2) Total Transactions: 5,780

3) On-Time Delivery Rate: 67.28%

4) Product Return Rate: 10.31%
